A few words about me

When it comes to my professional career, my main fields of interest are education, customer service and technology. My first encounter with coding was an attempt to combine those 3 areas of interest: using HTML and CSS, I created a simple website to share learning material with people learning French.

I believe technology should be about connecting people but also improving our lives by providing solutions to the challenges faced by our communities (such as the digital gap or environmental issues). Apart from coding, I enjoy creative activities such as sewing and any handcraft DIY involving fabrics.
